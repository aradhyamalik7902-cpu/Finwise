What is the Equity Market?

The equity market, or stock market, is where shares of publicly traded companies are bought and sold. It allows investors to own part of a company and potentially profit from its growth. Key players include exchanges like the NYSE and investors like you!

Problem Statement

The increasing participation of retail investors in the share market has not been matched by an equivalent increase in financial literacy. A significant number of investors lack awareness of basic share market principles, risk management strategies, and ethical investment practices. This gap between participation and knowledge poses a risk to individual financial stability and overall market efficiency. This study aims to analyze the level of financial literacy regarding the share market and identify measures to enhance investor awareness and decision-making skills.
